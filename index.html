01
The Return

A Bold Return to Cinema

Remi X Nneoma marks the powerful return of Five 2 Media Entertainment to the Nigerian film industry. A fresh and intimate drama inspired by the timeless biblical tale of Ruth and Naomi, the film explores love, grief, and the quiet resilience of the human spirit.

02
The Bond

Bound by Tragedy, Held by Love

The story follows the Amadi women, bound by shared tragedy. After the sudden loss of their beloved husband and father, Nneoma chooses to remain in Ikwerre, anchored to her roots and community. Her daughter-in-law Remi defies societal expectations and refuses to abandon her. Together, they navigate widowhood, cultural pressures, and the fragile path toward hope.

03
The Healing

A New Dawn After Heartbreak

In the stillness after heartbreak, Remi finds herself unexpectedly drawn toward a new love — opening a door for both women to rediscover joy and begin the long journey toward healing.

"A reflection on the quiet strength required to face life after tragedy — and the everyday resilience that defines so many women's experiences."

Distributors & Production

Bringing the Vision to Life

Production Company

A Nigerian-based film and production powerhouse known for acclaimed titles including On the Edge (Prime Video), Full Circle, Extra Mile (AFRIFF Official Selection), and A Night in 2005 (Prime Video). Five 2 Media returns with Remi X Nneoma, signaling a bold new direction in character-driven Nollywood storytelling.

Global Distribution

Secured global distribution rights for Remi X Nneoma, bringing this powerful Nigerian drama to audiences worldwide. The partnership ensures the film reaches cinemas across Nigeria and international markets, with a nationwide release slated for June 26, 2026 followed by global screenings.
